Our Story

Heritage meets modernity

Crafting Memories Since 1998

Columbina started as a humble family kitchen with a singular vision: to create the perfect Moon Cake. Over decades, our dedication to exceptional quality and artistic presentation transformed us into a premier destination for luxury desserts.

Today, under the guidance of our Master Bakers, we continue to innovate while honoring our roots, ensuring every bite is a celebration of taste and elegance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and ingredients

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add your favorites to the cart, and checkout via WhatsApp. You can also contact us directly for custom orders.

Do you deliver nationwide?

We offer local delivery for delicate pastries and nationwide courier shipping for selected items. Check the product details and shipping section for availability.

Can I request a custom flavor or gift packaging?

Yes! Send us a WhatsApp message or use the contact form to discuss custom flavors, packaging, and special event requests.

Do your products contain allergens?

Many of our products contain dairy, nuts, eggs, or gluten. Please review the ingredient details on each product page and contact us if you have specific allergy concerns.

Terms & Conditions

Our policies for ordering, payment, and service

Order Confirmation

All orders are confirmed once you receive a WhatsApp message from our team. Estimated delivery times may vary based on product type and location.

Payment

Payments are processed via the method agreed on WhatsApp. Prices listed on the site are subject to change until your order is confirmed.

Returns and Cancellations

Due to the perishable nature of our products, cancellations must be requested at least 24 hours before the scheduled preparation time. Returns are not accepted unless there is a defect or issue with the order.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information
